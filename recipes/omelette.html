Thai Omelette

Introduction:

This is a great hangover cure, snack or additional protein for your dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • a fair bit of oil
  • fish sauce
  • squeeze of lime
  • high smokepoint oil - I like rice bran because it tastes way better
  • Pinch of cornflour - or leave out, I never add it

Equipment

A wok is best here, but if you don't have one then I often use my trustiest frying pan. Use whatever you have.

  • Wok or pan

Method:

  1. Whisk your eggs with a fork or, obviously, a whisk.
  2. Add the rest of your ingredients minus the oil and whisk the hell out of it. You want it fluffy as all fuck.
  3. Heat your oil until smoking, you want a fair bit of oil for this, dont worry about calories, the aim is to enjoy your life while eating this.
  4. If there's been some time between steps one and two, give your eggs a further whisk.
  5. Move the egg around somewhat to prevent sticking. It'll bubble like crazy.
  6. MOnce the bottom is just below golden, flip it gracefully please do not splash yourself.
  7. Once golden on both sides, remove from the wok gracefully and put on a paper towel.
  8. Eat with chilli sauce and/or my sticky rice recipe..
